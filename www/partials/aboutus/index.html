About Us - Professional Automation and Mechanical Design Lab

Evottek Automation Lab

Evottek is a professional automation and mechanical design lab at the service of the transformation industry

Constituted by a team of specialized engineers with over fifteen years of experience, Evottek starts in 2010 as a professional lab dedicated to automation, mechanical design, and machining at the service of the transformation industry.

In Evottek we offer precise solutions based on the analyses conducted by our experts, who always present the best answer to your needs with the guarantee and confidence of facilitating your industrial processes.

What we do Solutions that facilitate industrial processes

We are a company dedicated to seek solutions that facilitate industrial processes by elaborating or modifying specific machinery to improve production, reduce times, or simplify the operator´s work.

In Evottek we provide a comprehensive service in which our engineers can design, program and assemble from an outside design, or develop the machining of projects as required by your company.

More than 15 years of experience in Mechanical Design and Control Engineering

Our vision is to be positioned as one of the leading companies in the field based on the quality of our service, not only in the Baja California region, but to expand throughout the northeast of Mexico and southern United States to meet the automation needs in the industrial sector.
