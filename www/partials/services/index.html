How we work

We deliver Solutions

In Evottek we offer solutions. After analyzing your case, we engage in seeking results that meet your needs as well as the industrial requirements. We are committed to provide follow-up to our work to guarantee the functionality and quality of our equipment.

Once the problem has been defined, our staff will be in charge of developing and implementing a plan that solves and facilitates your procedures through the following steps:

Information meeting

To have a project´s detailed information meeting: gathering data, needs and providing proposals for our client.

Design review

Suggested date based on of information meeting in which our team will present the machine´s design, technical details (measurements, material for manufacturing) and make adjustments or review the design, depending on our client´s needs.

Manufacturing and delivery

Upon of the approval of the design, a delivery date is provided and we begin the manufacturing of the product.

Debugging and follow-up

Once the machine is manufactured, we test and debug the system to ensure its proper functioning. Our job does not end at delivery, at Evottek, your satisfaction is of the outmost importance, that is why we provide follow-up service for each of our products.
